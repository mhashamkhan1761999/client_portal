## SEO

Project title: Local SEO Growth for Dental Clinic
Client or niche: Dental Clinic
Category: SEO

Short case-study summary:
The clinic had low local visibility and was not getting enough qualified calls from nearby patients. We improved local SEO, optimized service pages, fixed on-page issues, and strengthened the Google Business Profile.

What we did:
Audited technical SEO issues and fixed missing metadata
Optimized service pages for local buyer-intent keywords
Updated Google Business Profile categories, services, and descriptions
Created local content around dental services and nearby areas

The clinic started ranking better for local dental searches and received more qualified calls from nearby patients.

Generated result:
The SEO campaign improved local visibility by targeting high-intent dental keywords and strengthening the Google Business Profile. Organic traffic and local search impressions increased, creating more qualified calls from nearby patients.
